Makroni Basah

Makroni Basah is a popular Indonesian street snack made from macaroni cooked until soft and served with simple seasonings. This snack is commonly found at street food stalls or small local shops.

Ingredients

  • Macaroni
  • Oil
  • Seasoning
  • Water

Steps

  1. Boil water and add a little oil
  2. Add the macaroni and cook until soft
  3. Remove, drain, and set aside
  4. Add a little oil to the pan
  5. Add the cooked macaroni
  6. Turn on the heat, then add seasoning and stir well
  7. Ready to be served!
