Seblak

Seblak is Sundanese of indonesian local food that spicy and savory

Ingredients

  • Kerupuk
  • White & Green onion
  • Chilli
  • Aromatic ginger
  • Oil
  • Eggs
  • Seasoning
  • Water

Steps

  1. Slice green onion into pieces
  2. Crush chilli, white onion and aromatic ginger together as the main spice
  3. Add that spices to oil ready pan
  4. Turn the fire, wait untill smell then add the water (dont much, just enough same as how much kerupuk will drowned) also put the eggs together
  5. When the water starting hot and scrumble eggs cooked, add kerupuk and the green onion
  6. Finally, leave it as kerupuk has cooked and the water with spices are absorbed with them together
  7. Ready to served!
